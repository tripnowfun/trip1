Zatoka

The two-storey hotel in Zatoka was built in 2021. The guests of the resort are invited to rent comfortable accommodation in Zatoka on the seashore.

From any point of the hotel, gorgeous views of the sea open up. Comfortable and cozy environment for a family vacation.

For the convenience of guests, a modern kitchen is equipped for self-catering, there are gazebos.

Along the promenade near the hotel there is an alley with many cafes and retail outlets. There are also a couple of nightclubs and beach entertainment facilities. A water park is a 10-minute walk away.

Guests can park their cars in a secured area.
